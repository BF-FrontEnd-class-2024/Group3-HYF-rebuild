CORE PROGRAM

—



We offer an 8-month full-stack web development program once a year
in both Brussels and Antwerp. Our program is free. We teach refugees, newcomers and allophone
job seekers with limited access to further education or the job market in
Belgium. The course is open to anyone staying in Belgium.
A technical background is not necessary if you want to apply to
HackYourFuture core program.
Before you apply, check the list below to ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria:

The application is closed

  • You're + 18 years old
  • You're extremely motivated to start a career in web development.
  • You speak English at an intermediate level.
  • You have to spend at least 30 hours per week on Sunday classes, homework and self-learning.
  • You can attend on-site sessions on Sundays from 12:00 - 16:00.
  • You can commit to the program for the full 8 months.

Women are strongly encouraged to apply.
The new class registration will start in 2024

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

—

We provide an environment for self-directed learning of the fundamentals of full-stack web development. We are not just a school, we are a learning community,
supported by a network of skilled volunteer coaches and mentors from the tech industry. HackYourFuture core program requires 30 hours of independent study per
week, of which 4 hours are on-site workshops on Sundays. You are responsible for your own learning. Together, we will discover state-of-the-art tools and frameworks,
industry best-practices, and experience working collaboratively in a modern development team.

THE APPLICATION PROCESS

Applying to our program consists of four important steps.

—

Step 1 - The application

To access our application form, please click here. While completing the form, you will need to submit a motivation letter that addresses specific questions and your CV. It is important to ensure that both documents are prepared carefully. Once you have submitted the form, you will receive a confirmation email from us.

Step 2 - The explore module

To familiarise yourself with being a HackYourFuture student, we ask you to complete the introductory module. The module is your first look at what being a developer is, and at how the HackYourFuture program works. We advise you to start this module right now as we expect you to have this knowledge to complete the assignment. There are tips at the end of the module on what to do to increase your chances of succeeding.

Step 3 - The technical assignment

You will receive your tech assignment by email. You will have around 3 weeks to complete it. The assignment consists of implementing all the knowledge you gained in the introductory module. It is important that you take this seriously. We advise you to block these 3 weeks in your agenda and spend at least 4 hours a day on it. An online meeting is organized in case you have any questions

Step 4 -The interview

If you perform well on the technical assignment, you will receive an invitation to an face to face interview. During the interview, we will talk about your background, motivation, and the technical assignment. Be sure to be well-prepared for it! In case you are not selected for our upcoming class, we will direct you to our partners when relevant and you may reapply for the next opportunity!

5 MONTHS FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

HackYourFuture, in collaboration with Bruxelles Formation, is launching a
5-month full-time Front-end Development training that includes an
internship opportunity upon completion.
You will learn how to create a responsive website using HTML to structure
content, CSS to handle visual style, and JavaScript to develop interactive
experiences. You will also learn React, a “must-have” skill for front-end
developers. Before you apply, please check the list below to ensure that you meet the
eligibility criteria:

  • You're + 18 years old
  • You are registered with Actiris, VDAB, or Le Forem
  • You speak English at an intermediate level.
  • You're extremely motivated to start a career in web development.

The new class registration will start in 2024




2 MONTH BACK-END DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

HackYourFuture, in collaboration with Bruxelles Formation, is organising 2-month full-
time Back-end Development training that includes an internship opportunity upon
completion. In this training you will learn Node.js, one of the most popular server-side platforms. The
training covers all the basics of Node.js and its architectural concepts to help you
create your own web applications using Node.js.
Before you apply, please ensure that you meet the following conditions:

  • You're + 18 years old
  • You are registered with Actiris, VDAB, or Le Forem
  • You speak English at an intermediate level.
  • You're extremely motivated to start a career in web development.
  • You should have a working understanding of Javascript, HTML and CSS

The new class registration will start on 2024, May, 08
